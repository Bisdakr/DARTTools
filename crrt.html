Patient
Modality
CVVHDF = continuous venovenous hemodiafiltration (combined diffusion + convection — most common). CVVH = convection only with substitution fluid. CVVHD = diffusion only with countercurrent dialysate. SCUF = slow continuous ultrafiltration (fluid removal only, no solute target).
Prescription
Common: 100% post-filter (max clearance) · 30/70 pre/post (anti-clot balance) · 100% pre-filter (max anti-clot, lowest clearance)
Total effluent splits between dialysate (diffusion arm) and replacement (convection arm). 50/50 is the most common balanced setting.
Anticoagulation
Advanced (optional)
Hematocrit · Filter set · FFR target
Filtration Fraction = Effluent / Plasma flow. KDIGO recommends keeping FFR < 25% to reduce filter clotting from hemoconcentration.
