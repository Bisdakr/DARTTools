Informational only — not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. All outputs are estimates based on published apheresis, hematology, and nephrology formulas (ASFA categorization, Nadler equation, KDOQI/KDIGO thresholds, ISPD adequacy targets, and standard physiologic relationships). Every result must be independently verified by a licensed clinician against the patient's clinical context, physician orders, current guidelines, and institutional protocols. Outputs may differ from calculations performed by commercial apheresis or dialysis devices.
